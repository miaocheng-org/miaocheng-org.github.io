## Overview

The Affective Motion Dataset is a comprehensive collection of motion capture data specifically designed for emotion recognition research. It contains high-quality skeleton sequences from multiple actors expressing a range of emotional states.

## Features

- 10,000+ motion sequences
- 8 emotion categories
- Multiple camera angles
- Detailed annotations
